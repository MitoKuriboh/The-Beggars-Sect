## Naming Convention

**Chinese-inspired names with meaning:**
- Elders: Surname + title (Elder Chen, Elder Wu)
- Beggars: "Brother/Sister" + name OR nickname
- Thugs: Descriptive nicknames (Razor, Scarred Jin)
- Spartans: Military titles + name (Commander Vex)
- Lone Wolves: Cryptic titles (The Hollow One)

## Beggars Sect Elders

*Masters of the sect who teach Li Wei the true path of the beggar.*

---

### Elder Chen (陈 - "old, ancient")
**Role:** First Elder / Primary Mentor
**Faction:** Beggars Sect
**Chapters:** 1, 2, 3

**Appearance:**
An elderly man with kind eyes and a warm smile. Wears patched but clean robes. His hands are calloused from decades of palm training. A bamboo staff he rarely uses. Long grey beard, tied loosely. Moves with surprising grace for his apparent age.

**Personality:**
- **Speech:** Gentle, patient, uses metaphors and stories
- **Traits:** Wise, observant, quietly humorous
- **Philosophy:** "Strength through understanding, not force"
- **Quirk:** Often speaks in riddles, then laughs at Li Wei's confusion

**Relationship to Li Wei:**
- Recognizes Li Wei's hidden potential immediately
- Becomes primary teacher and father figure
- Tests Li Wei through indirect lessons
- Believes Li Wei may be the one to restore the sect's glory

**Techniques Taught:**
1. **Flowing Strike** - Chapter 1
2. **Stream Palm** - Chapter 1
3. **Ripple Guard** - Chapter 1
4. **Beggar's Feint** - Chapter 1

**Key Dialogue:**

*First Meeting:*
> "You have the eyes of someone searching, young one. Tell me... what are you looking for?"

*Beginning Training:*
> "The palm is not a weapon. It is a conversation. Let me teach you to speak."

*After First Victory:*
> "Good. But remember—defeating an enemy is easy. Understanding why you fought... that is the true challenge."

*Proud Moment:*
> "Ha! You've grown beyond my humble teachings. Soon, you won't need this old man at all."

*End of Chapter 1:*
> "Razor was just the beginning. Darker things stir in this city. Train hard, Li Wei. You'll need everything I can teach you."

**Story Function:**
- Tutorial guide through Chapter 1
- Introduces beggar philosophy
- Provides emotional anchor
- Foreshadows Li Wei's greater destiny

### Elder Wu (吴 - "military, martial")
**Role:** Second Elder / Defense Master
**Faction:** Beggars Sect
**Chapters:** 1, 2, 3

**Appearance:**
A stocky, muscular woman in her 50s with short grey hair. Scars on her forearms from years of blocking attacks. Wears practical, dark robes. Stern resting face that softens rarely. Missing two fingers on her left hand (old injury).

**Personality:**
- **Speech:** Blunt, direct, few words
- **Traits:** Stoic, disciplined, secretly caring
- **Philosophy:** "Endure first. Strike second. Regret never."
- **Quirk:** Responds to questions with questions, makes students figure things out

**Relationship to Li Wei:**
- Initially skeptical of Li Wei's potential
- Becomes impressed by his persistence
- Teaches through harsh training
- Respects actions over words

**Techniques Taught:**
1. **Weathered Palm** - Chapter 1
2. **Steadfast Guard** - Chapter 1
3. **Iron Palm** - Chapter 2
4. **Rising Dragon** - Chapter 2

**Key Dialogue:**

*First Meeting:*
> "Chen thinks you're special. I think you're soft. Prove him right."

*Training:*
> "Again. Again. AGAIN. Pain is temporary. Weakness is forever."

*After Progress:*
> "...Adequate."
> (This is high praise from Wu)

*Showing Respect:*
> "You don't give up. That's rarer than talent. Perhaps Chen was right after all."

*Before Boss Fight:*
> "Words won't save you. Hesitation won't save you. Only your palms can save you now. GO."

**Story Function:**
- Provides contrast to Elder Chen's gentleness
- Teaches defensive mastery
- Represents the hardship beggars face
- Character development through earned respect

### Elder Mei (梅 - "plum blossom")
**Role:** Third Elder / Offense Master
**Faction:** Beggars Sect
**Chapters:** 2, 3

**Appearance:**
A thin, wiry woman in her 40s with wild black hair streaked with white. Intense, hungry eyes. Wears deliberately tattered robes. Moves in sudden bursts, never still. Chews on dried meat constantly.

**Personality:**
- **Speech:** Rapid, excited, interrupts herself
- **Traits:** Eccentric, passionate, unpredictable
- **Philosophy:** "Hunger drives. Hunger teaches. Embrace your hunger."
- **Quirk:** Gets distracted mid-sentence, starts new topics randomly

**Relationship to Li Wei:**
- Fascinated by Li Wei's mysterious origin
- Teaches through chaos and improvisation
- Pushes Li Wei to embrace his aggressive side
- Sees Li Wei as the sect's future champion

**Techniques Taught:**
1. **Ravenous Palm** - Chapter 2
2. **Feral Combo** - Chapter 2
3. **Desperate Strike** - (Discovered, Mei explains it)

**Key Dialogue:**

*First Meeting:*
> "Chen's student! Wu says you're tough, Chen says you're clever, I say—are you HUNGRY? Because I'm going to teach you to BITE!"

*Training:*
> "No no NO! You're holding back! A hungry wolf doesn't hesitate! It STRIKES! Again—but meaner this time!"

*Excited:*
> "YES! Did you feel that? That rush? That's hunger becoming power! Oh, you're going to be FUN to teach!"

*Philosophical Moment:*
> "The others think hunger is a weakness. But we know, don't we? Hunger is what keeps us alive. What keeps us fighting. Never be satisfied, Li Wei. Satisfaction is death."

*Before Chapter 3:*
> "Something's coming. Something big. My stomach tells me. Train the hungry techniques, Li Wei. You'll need teeth before this is over."

**Story Function:**
- Introduces aggressive playstyle option
- Adds energy and humor
- Represents beggar desperation turned to strength
- Foreshadows final conflict

### Old Dao (道 - "the way, path")
**Role:** Initial Contact / Guide
**Faction:** Beggars Sect
**Chapters:** 1

**Appearance:**
Ancient, hunched beggar with milky white eyes (blind). Wears layered rags. Carries a wooden bowl. Smells of incense. Seems feeble but moves with purpose.

**Personality:**
- **Speech:** Cryptic, philosophical, ancient
- **Traits:** Mysterious, knowing, patient
- **Philosophy:** "The path finds those who need it."
- **Quirk:** Seems to see despite blindness, knows things he shouldn't

**Relationship to Li Wei:**
- First beggar to approach Li Wei
- Guides Li Wei to the sect
- Disappears after introduction (mysterious)
- May know more than he reveals

**Key Dialogue:**

*First Words:*
> "You're lost. Not geographically—the other kind. Come. I know people who help the lost."

*Walking:*
> "You don't remember how you got here, do you? That's alright. Some journeys begin in forgetting."

*At Sect Entrance:*
> "This is where I leave you. The elders will decide the rest. Remember, young one—the path chose you. Not the other way around."

*Final Words:*
> "We'll meet again. When you understand what you are."

**Story Function:**
- Narrative device to introduce sect
- Creates mystery about Li Wei's origin
- Potential future plot hook
- Establishes mystical elements

### Brother Feng (风 - "wind")
**Role:** Fellow Disciple / Friend
**Faction:** Beggars Sect
**Chapters:** 1, 2, 3

**Appearance:**
Young man, early 20s, slightly older than Li Wei. Thin but wiry. Friendly face with a chipped tooth. Wears a tattered hat he's proud of. Quick to smile, quicker to laugh.

**Personality:**
- **Speech:** Casual, joking, supportive
- **Traits:** Loyal, optimistic, slightly envious
- **Philosophy:** "We're all in this together"
- **Quirk:** Makes up terrible puns about beggar life

**Relationship to Li Wei:**
- Becomes Li Wei's first friend in the sect
- Training partner and sparring buddy
- Looks up to Li Wei's rapid progress
- Provides emotional support and comic relief

**Key Dialogue:**

*First Meeting:*
> "New guy! I'm Feng. Don't worry, the elders look scary but they're mostly bark. Mostly. Elder Wu's bite is pretty bad actually."

*Training Together:*
> "How are you learning so fast?! I've been here two years and you're already—you know what, I'm not jealous. I'm NOT. Okay maybe a little."

*Supportive:*
> "Hey. Whatever's bothering you about your past... you've got a family now. We beggars stick together."

*Before Big Fight:*
> "Go get 'em, Li Wei. And try not to die. I'd miss having someone to lose to in sparring."

*After Victory:*
> "THAT WAS AMAZING! Tell me everything! Did you use the thing? The whoosh-whoosh thing Elder Wu taught?"

**Story Function:**
- Humanizes the sect
- Provides exposition through questions
- Comic relief
- Shows Li Wei has something to protect

### Razor
**Role:** Chapter 1 Boss
**Faction:** Urban Thugs (Leader)
**Chapters:** 1

**Appearance:**
Mid-30s, lean and sharp-featured. Slicked-back hair. Wears a leather jacket over bare chest. Has a distinctive scar across his cheek (self-inflicted for reputation). Gold teeth. Carries no visible weapons—his hands are enough.

**Personality:**
- **Speech:** Arrogant, mocking, street slang
- **Traits:** Cruel, ambitious, insecure underneath
- **Philosophy:** "Strength takes what it wants"
- **Quirk:** Refers to himself in third person when angry

**Relationship to Li Wei:**
- Sees Li Wei as insignificant pest
- Becomes obsessed after defeat
- Represents the brutal side of the streets
- First real test of Li Wei's growth

**Key Dialogue:**

*Before Fight:*
> "A beggar? You're here to challenge ME? Razor runs these streets! Boys, teach this—wait. Where'd my boys go?"

*Mid-Fight:*
> "Lucky hit! LUCKY HIT! Razor doesn't lose to BEGGARS!"

*Defeated:*
> "This... this isn't over. You hear me?! RAZOR WILL REMEMBER THIS!"

**Story Function:**
- First major victory
- Proves Li Wei's growth
- Opens gang territory
- Sets up potential revenge (sequel hook)

### Commander Vex
**Role:** Chapter 2 Boss
**Faction:** Spartans (Commander)
**Chapters:** 2

**Appearance:**
Late 40s, tall and imposing. Military-perfect posture. Clean-shaved head. Cold, calculating eyes. Wears Spartan officer armor—polished and precise. Carries a ceremonial blade he doesn't need.

**Personality:**
- **Speech:** Formal, measured, intelligent
- **Traits:** Disciplined, tactical, honorable in his way
- **Philosophy:** "Order through strength. Peace through control."
- **Quirk:** Analyzes everything, even emotions

**Relationship to Li Wei:**
- Initially dismissive of "vagrant threat"
- Becomes genuinely interested after combat
- Respects Li Wei's skill while opposing his existence
- May become reluctant ally in future

**Key Dialogue:**

*Before Fight:*
> "So you're the beggar causing disorder in my district. I expected... more. Surrender now and I'll ensure fair treatment."

*Mid-Fight:*
> "Fascinating. Your technique is unorthodox. Inefficient, but... effective. Let me show you what true discipline achieves."

*Defeated:*
> "Impossible. Defeated by a... no. You're no ordinary beggar. Who ARE you? What IS this technique?"

**Story Function:**
- Escalates conflict to authority level
- Tests Li Wei against organized opposition
- Opens high district
- Plants seeds of larger conspiracy

### The Hollow One
**Role:** Chapter 3 Boss (Final)
**Faction:** Lone Wolf (Mysterious)
**Chapters:** 3

**Appearance:**
Ageless—could be 30 or 60. Androgynous features. Completely bald. Wears simple grey robes, no adornment. Eyes that seem to look through you. Moves like water, soundless. No expression.

**Personality:**
- **Speech:** Minimalist, profound, questions more than states
- **Traits:** Detached, seeking, beyond normal concerns
- **Philosophy:** "Mastery is unlearning. Perfection is emptiness."
- **Quirk:** Speaks in koans, waits for answers that don't come

**Relationship to Li Wei:**
- Sees Li Wei as potential worthy opponent
- Tests Li Wei's understanding, not just strength
- Holds key to Li Wei's future (Dog Beating Staff)
- Neither enemy nor ally—a mirror

**Key Dialogue:**

*Before Fight:*
> "Another seeker. Tell me, beggar... what do you fight for?"

*During Fight:*
> "You rely on your elders' teachings. But where is YOUR technique? Show me what YOU have created."

*If Winning:*
> "Yes... YES! This is what I sought! A worthy opponent at last!"

*Defeated:*
> "At last... someone who understands. The Dog Beating Staff... it's hidden in the mountains. Find it. Master it. Then... find me again. I'll be waiting."

**Story Function:**
- Climactic challenge
- Tests everything Li Wei has learned
- Delivers cliffhanger ending
- Sets up sequel/continuation

### Scarred Jin
**Role:** Recurring Rival
**Faction:** Urban Thugs
**Chapters:** 1, 2

**Appearance:**
Early 20s, heavyset and mean. Half his face is burned (origin unknown). Shaved head. Wears gang colors proudly. Always cracking his knuckles.

**Personality:**
- **Speech:** Crude, threatening, simple
- **Traits:** Vengeful, loyal (to Razor), persistent
- **Philosophy:** "Hit harder. Hit more."
- **Quirk:** Never forgets a slight, holds grudges forever

**Relationship to Li Wei:**
- Razor's top enforcer
- Personally humiliated by Li Wei
- Returns multiple times seeking revenge
- Comic relief villain

**Key Dialogue:**

*First Encounter:*
> "Boss wants you gone. Nothing personal. Well, maybe a little personal now."

*Second Encounter:*
> "YOU! I've been looking for you! Razor's gonna love me when I bring your teeth!"

*Third Encounter:*
> "Why... won't... you... LOSE?!"

**Story Function:**
- Recurring mini-boss
- Shows Li Wei's progression (easier each time)
- Comic relief through persistent failure
- Represents gang's declining threat

## Voice Guidelines

### Elders
- **Chen:** Warm, grandfatherly, uses nature metaphors
- **Wu:** Terse, military-like, imperative sentences
- **Mei:** Energetic, scattered, food metaphors

### Beggars
- **Casual tone**, contractions, street-smart
- **Supportive** of each other
- **Self-deprecating humor** about beggar life

### Thugs
- **Crude**, threatening, simple vocabulary
- **Bravado** that crumbles under pressure
- **Street slang** and insults

### Spartans
- **Formal**, military terminology
- **Longer sentences**, proper grammar
- **Respectful** even to enemies (until proven worthy of disrespect)

### Lone Wolves
- **Cryptic**, philosophical
- **Questions** rather than statements
- **Minimal words**, maximum impact
