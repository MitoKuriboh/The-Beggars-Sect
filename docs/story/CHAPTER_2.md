## Scene 2.1: Growing Shadows

**Type:** Cutscene & Exploration
**Location:** Beggar's Corner, then Lower Streets
**Duration:** 15 minutes

### Opening: Three Weeks Later

```
[FADE IN - Night at Beggar's Corner]

[Li Wei meditates beneath the Beggar's Pine]
[The jade pendant glows faintly in the darkness]

NARRATOR TEXT:
Three weeks since Razor.
Three weeks of training.
Three weeks of growing stronger.
But also three weeks of dreams.

[DREAM SEQUENCE - Brief flash]

[White room. Sterile walls. A young Li Wei strapped to a table]

VOICE: (clinical)
"Subject 17's chi flow remains inverted.
Increase the suppression dosage."

YOUNG LI WEI: (struggling)
Please... it hurts...

VOICE: (cold)
"Pain is necessary for calibration."

[Li Wei gasps awake]

[Elder Chen sits nearby, watching]

ELDER CHEN: The dreams again?

LI WEI: (touching pendant)
They're getting clearer. I can almost
see faces now.

ELDER CHEN: Memory returns when it's ready.
Or when something forces it.

[He stands]

ELDER CHEN: Speaking of which. We have
a problem.

[Scene continues to exploration]
```

### The Problem

```
[Elder's Quarters - All three Elders present]

ELDER WU: Spartan patrols have increased.
They're asking questions. About a beggar
who caused trouble with the gangs.

ELDER MEI: (to Li Wei)
Your fame precedes you.

[Varies by Ch1 path:]

[If BLADE path dominant:]

ELDER CHEN: Your victory over Razor didn't

more carefully now.

help. Or it might make you a bigger target.

[If SHADOW path dominant:]

be fine. Step into the light...

activated recovery protocols.

ELDER MEI: (breaking tension)

to train with me! That changes now.

[She grins ferally]

ELDER MEI: Time to learn the HUNGRY stance.

**Location:** Li Wei's Mind / Past

```
[NIGHT - Li Wei sleeps]

               CALIBRATION INITIATIVE

Double-check everything.

RESEARCHER 2: Director, he's the first
subject to survive with stable inverse chi.
Subjects 1 through 16 all...

DR. MAE: (protectively)

RESEARCHER 1: (coldly)

[FLASH FORWARD - 10 years later]

                    2 YEARS AGO
               THE SAME FACILITY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[His chi FLARES - visible, inverted, wrong]

[The jade pendant around his neck glows]

DIRECTOR SHEN: (watching through glass)

RESEARCHER: Imagine if we removed the suppression.

DIRECTOR SHEN: We tried. Three times.

[He pulls up files on a screen - destroyed rooms,

DIRECTOR SHEN: Every time the pendant is

[FLASH FORWARD - Months ago]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    3 MONTHS AGO
               THE ESCAPE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[Multiple electrodes attached]
[The pendant has been removed for "calibration"]

DR. MAE: Director, his chi readings are

[Everything goes white]

[Researchers lie unconscious or worse]
